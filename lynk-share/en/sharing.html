SHARE THE LOVE

Simple car sharing.

Sometimes you need a little help from your friends. That’s is why we made it easy to share your car. Use the platform to share with the Lynk & Co community members with just a few taps in our mobile app.

The world needs more sharing.

Cars are only in use 4% of the time. The rest of the time, they’re just... sitting there. But, what if we all shared a little more? 

  • Sustainable mobility for all. By sharing cars, we can reduce the number of individual cars on the road and use our urban space more efficiently... because do we really want more parking lots?
  • Lower costs. Sign up for a free membership and you’ll only pay when you actually borrow a car. If you’re lending out your car, you can set your own prices and lower your monthly costs.
  • And of course, with the Lynk & Co sharing platform, it’s super, super simple. 

How does car sharing work at Lynk & Co? 

Whether you’re lending out your car or borrowing from another member, we’re working to make car sharing as easy as possible. These are the basics, but keep an eye out for more updates and info.

Borrowers

If you have a membership with a car, then you can share it with members of the Lynk & Co community.

You’ll set your own prices, schedule, and where the car is picked up and dropped off. Plus, you can share access to your car right from your Lynk & Co app using a digital key.
Worried about your car? The sharing platform features a star rating system so that you know who’s using it.

P.S. No matter what membership you sign up for, you never have to share your car if you don’t want to. It’s 100% your choice.
